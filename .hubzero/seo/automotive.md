# Automotive SEO

Automotive websites generate visibility by helping customers research vehicles, compare options, and schedule enquiries or services.

SEO should support the entire ownership journey rather than only vehicle sales.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Compare vehicles
- Find dealerships
- Book servicing
- Research specifications
- Request quotations

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Vehicle models
- Manufacturers
- Dealerships
- Servicing
- Parts
- Financing

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Inventory
- Services
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Vehicle Detail
- Financing
- Parts
- Offers
- Blog

---

# Information Architecture

Vehicles should be organized logically by manufacturer, model, and category.

Support ownership with dedicated service resources.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Inventory
- Vehicle pages
- Financing
- Services
- Parts

Support buyers throughout the research process.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- AutoDealer
- Product

- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Offer

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Buying guides
- Vehicle comparisons
- Maintenance advice
- Ownership tips
- Industry news

Support commercial pages with educational resources.

---

# Local SEO

Critical.

Include:

- Dealership locations
- Service centers
- Maps
- Contact information

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Reviews
- Certifications
- Warranty information
- Dealer credentials

Medium Priority

- Awards
- Partnerships
- Community involvement

---

# Technical Considerations

- Fast inventory pages
- Image optimization
- Structured data
- Mobile-first
- Strong metadata

---

# Common Mistakes

- Duplicate inventory
- Poor vehicle descriptions
- Hidden pricing
- Weak local optimization
- Thin service pages
