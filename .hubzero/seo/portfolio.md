# Portfolio SEO

Portfolio websites earn visibility by demonstrating expertise through real work.

SEO should emphasize credibility, project outcomes, and professional reputation rather than high publishing volume.

Authority should come from evidence.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find professionals
- Evaluate previous work
- Assess expertise
- Verify credibility
- Contact the creator

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Profession
- Services
- Specializations
- Projects
- Location
- Personal or company name

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Portfolio
- Project Pages
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Blog
- Services
- Testimonials
- Resume
- Awards

---

# Information Architecture

Every significant project should have its own page.

Projects should be organized logically by category, industry, or expertise.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Projects
- Services
- Articles
- About
- Contact

Case studies should reinforce service offerings.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Person or Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- CreativeWork
- Article

---

# Content Strategy

Focus on:

- Case studies
- Project breakdowns
- Technical articles
- Process explanations
- Industry insights

Quality matters more than quantity.

---

# Local SEO

Useful for freelancers, agencies, and consultants serving regional markets.

Include location information where appropriate.

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Completed projects
- Client testimonials
- Measurable results
- Awards
- Certifications

Medium Priority

- Publications
- Open source
- Speaking engagements

---

# Technical Considerations

- Optimized project pages
- Strong image optimization
- Metadata
- Open Graph
- Fast loading
- Accessible galleries

---

# Common Mistakes

- Thin project descriptions
- Too many low-quality projects
- Missing outcomes
- Generic marketing copy
- Poor image optimization

---

# Success Metrics

- Qualified enquiries
- Project page engagement
- Organic traffic
- Contact conversions
- Returning visitors
