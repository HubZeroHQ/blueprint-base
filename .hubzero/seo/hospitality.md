# Hospitality SEO

Hospitality websites earn visibility by helping potential guests confidently evaluate experiences before making reservations.

SEO should prioritize accurate information, compelling experiences, and frictionless booking journeys.

Trust should be established before asking visitors to reserve.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find accommodation
- Discover restaurants
- Compare venues
- Check availability
- Make reservations

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Hotels
- Resorts
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Venues
- Amenities
- Local attractions
- Locations

---

# Information Architecture

Accommodation, dining, or experiences should remain the primary navigation focus.

Reservation paths should always remain easily accessible.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy
