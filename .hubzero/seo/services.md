# Services SEO

Service websites compete by demonstrating expertise, solving problems, and attracting high-intent visitors.

SEO should focus on matching customer problems with clear service pages.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Solve a problem
- Compare providers
- Understand pricing
- Evaluate expertise
- Request a quotation

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Service names
- Problems
- Industries
- Locations
- Solutions

Target intent rather than isolated keywords.

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Services
- Individual Service Pages
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Pricing
- Industries
- Case Studies
- FAQs
- Blog

---

# Information Architecture

Each major service should have its own dedicated page.

Avoid combining unrelated services onto a single page.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Services
- Case Studies
- Blog Articles
- FAQs
- Contact

Educational content should naturally support service pages.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization
- Service
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- FAQPage
- LocalBusiness

---

# Content Strategy

Produce content answering real customer questions.

Examples:

- Service guides
- Comparisons
- Common mistakes
- Industry insights
- Case studies

---

# Local SEO

Often critical.

Prioritize:

- Location pages
- LocalBusiness schema
- Geographic relevance
- Accurate contact information

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Reviews
- Case studies
- Certifications
- Portfolio
- Testimonials

Medium Priority

- Awards
- Partnerships
- Team expertise

---

# Technical Considerations

- Fast pages
- Mobile optimization
- Clean URLs
- Strong metadata
- Internal linking
- Accessibility

---

# Common Mistakes

- Multiple services on one page
- Thin content
- Weak trust signals
- Duplicate location pages
- Generic testimonials

---

# Success Metrics

- Service enquiries
- Quote requests
- Organic traffic
- Local visibility
- Conversion rate
