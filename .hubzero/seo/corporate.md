# Corporate SEO

Corporate websites build visibility by establishing trust, authority, and a clear understanding of the business.

SEO should support long-term brand discovery rather than short-term keyword targeting.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Learn about the company
- Understand capabilities
- Evaluate credibility
- Contact the business
- Verify legitimacy

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Company name
- Industry
- Solutions
- Capabilities
- Expertise
- Location
- Brand terms

Focus on topical authority rather than individual keywords.

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- About
- Contact
- Services
- Industries

Recommended

- Leadership
- Careers
- Case Studies
- Resources
- FAQs

---

# Information Architecture

Navigation should communicate:

- Who the company is
- What it does
- Who it serves
- Why it is credible
- How to get in touch

Pages should remain focused and avoid unnecessary overlap.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Services
- Industries
- Case Studies
- Resources
- Team

Every important page should be reachable within a few clicks.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Person
- FAQPage

---

# Content Strategy

Content should demonstrate expertise.

Examples include:

- Industry insights
- Case studies
- Technical articles
- Company news
- Frequently asked questions

Quality is more important than publishing frequency.

---

# Local SEO

If the business serves a geographic region:

- Maintain accurate business information.
- Include office locations.
- Create location-specific pages when justified.

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Real team members
- Client testimonials
- Case studies
- Certifications
- Awards

Medium Priority

- Partnerships
- Publications
- Press coverage

---

# Technical Considerations

- Strong metadata
- Canonical URLs
- XML sitemap
- Open Graph
- Fast loading
- Mobile-first
- Accessibility

---

# Common Mistakes

- Generic marketing copy
- Thin service pages
- Duplicate content
- Weak About page
- No case studies
- Poor internal linking

---

# Success Metrics

- Qualified enquiries
- Organic traffic
- Branded searches
- Contact conversions
- Time on site
