# Blog SEO

Blog websites build long-term organic growth by consistently publishing valuable, trustworthy, and well-organized content.

SEO should establish topical authority rather than chase individual keywords.

Every article should strengthen the overall knowledge base.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Learn
- Research
- Solve problems
- Compare ideas
- Discover new information

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Educational topics
- Tutorials
- Guides
- Comparisons
- Best practices
- Industry trends

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Categories
- Articles
- About

Recommended

- Authors
- Search
- Newsletter
- Tags
- Resources

---

# Information Architecture

Content should be organized into meaningful topic clusters.

Categories should reflect user interests rather than internal organization.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Related articles
- Categories
- Authors
- Evergreen guides
- Resource pages

Every article should naturally lead readers to additional relevant content.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- BlogPosting
- Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Person
- FAQPage

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Evergreen guides
- Tutorials
- Industry analysis
- Comparisons
- Opinion pieces

Prioritize long-term value over publishing frequency.

---

# Local SEO

Generally unnecessary unless serving a regional audience.

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Named authors
- Publication dates
- Last updated dates
- References

Medium Priority

- Expert contributors
- Editorial standards
- Reader engagement

---

# Technical Considerations

- Fast loading
- Strong metadata
- Structured headings
- Canonical URLs
- Image optimization

---

# Common Mistakes

- Thin articles
- Keyword stuffing
- Weak internal linking
- Duplicate topics
- Clickbait headlines

# Success Metrics

- Organic traffic
- Returning readers
