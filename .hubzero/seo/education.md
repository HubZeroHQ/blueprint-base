# Education SEO

Education websites build long-term visibility by helping prospective students, parents, and educators discover learning opportunities through accurate, accessible, and trustworthy information.

SEO should support informed decision-making throughout the enrollment journey.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Explore programs
- Compare institutions
- Learn admission requirements
- Discover courses
- Contact admissions

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Courses
- Programs
- Degrees
- Admissions
- Faculty
- Campus
- Student life

Focus on educational intent rather than promotional language.

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Programs
- Admissions
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Faculty
- Campus
- Events
- Student Resources
- News
- FAQs

---

# Information Architecture

Programs should be organized logically by department, qualification, or subject.

Admissions should always be easy to locate.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Programs
- Faculty
- Admissions
- Resources
- Events

Support every program page with relevant educational content.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- CollegeOrUniversity or EducationalOrganization
- Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Course
- FAQPage
- Article

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Course guides
- Admission information
- Student success stories
- Faculty highlights
- Educational resources
- Campus news

Educational value should always outweigh promotional messaging.

---

# Local SEO

Important for physical institutions.

Include:

- Campus locations
- Contact details
- Maps
- Visiting information

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Accreditation
- Faculty credentials
- Student outcomes
- Research
- Alumni success

Medium Priority

- Rankings
- Awards
- Partnerships

---

# Technical Considerations

- Accessible navigation
- Fast loading
- Mobile-first
- Structured data
- Strong metadata

---

# Common Mistakes

- Hidden admissions information
- Outdated course details
- Weak faculty pages
- Thin program descriptions
- Poor navigation

---

# Success Metrics

- Applications
- Information requests
- Program engagement
- Organic traffic
- Campus visit registrations
