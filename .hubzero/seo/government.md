# Government SEO

Government websites exist to make public information and services easy to discover, understand, and access.

SEO should improve citizen access rather than maximize traffic.

Information should remain accurate, accessible, and trustworthy.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Access government services
- Download forms
- Read announcements
- Find departments
- Contact officials

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Public services
- Government departments
- Forms
- Licenses
- Announcements
- Regulations

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Services
- Departments
- Contact
- News

Recommended

- Forms
- Documents
- FAQs
- Emergency Information
- Public Notices

---

# Information Architecture

Services should be organized around citizen needs rather than internal government structures.

Frequently used services should remain prominent.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Services
- Departments
- Documents
- News
- Contact

Support task completion through logical navigation.

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Public notices
- Policy updates
- Service guides
- Emergency information
- Community announcements

Accuracy should always outweigh publishing frequency.
