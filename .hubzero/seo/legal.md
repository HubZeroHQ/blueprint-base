# Legal SEO

Legal websites compete by demonstrating expertise, authority, and trust.

SEO should help prospective clients understand legal services while reducing uncertainty before making contact.

Educational content should establish credibility without providing misleading legal advice.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find legal representation
- Understand legal issues
- Compare law firms
- Schedule consultations
- Learn legal processes

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Practice areas
- Legal services
- Legal issues
- Attorney names
- Locations
- Legal resources

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Practice Areas
- Attorneys
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Case Results
- Resources
- FAQs
- Blog
- Consultation

---

# Information Architecture

Practice areas should organize the website.

Educational content should support legal service pages.

Attorney profiles should reinforce credibility.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Practice areas
- Attorneys
- Articles
- FAQs
- Consultation pages

Educational resources should naturally reinforce legal expertise.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization
- Attorney
- LegalService
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- FAQPage
- Article

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Legal guides
- Frequently asked questions
- Process explanations
- Regulatory updates
- Educational articles

Content should educate rather than replace professional legal advice.

---

# Local SEO

Critical.

Include:

- Office locations
- Practice areas by location
- Contact information
- Service regions

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Attorney profiles
- Bar memberships
- Certifications
- Awards
- Testimonials (where appropriate)

Medium Priority

- Publications
- Speaking engagements
- Community involvement

---

# Technical Considerations

- Strong metadata
- Accessibility
- Structured data
- Performance
- Secure contact forms

---

# Common Mistakes

- Generic practice pages
- Thin legal content
- Anonymous articles
- Weak attorney profiles
- Poor local optimization
