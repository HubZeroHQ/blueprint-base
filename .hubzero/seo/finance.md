# Finance SEO

Finance websites earn visibility through expertise, transparency, and trustworthy financial guidance.

SEO should prioritize educational value while communicating professionalism and regulatory responsibility.

Trust is the primary competitive advantage.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Understand financial products
- Compare options
- Learn financial concepts
- Find advisors
- Request consultations

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Financial services
- Investments
- Loans
- Insurance
- Tax
- Planning
- Advisory

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Services
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Resources
- Calculators
- Blog
- FAQs
- Case Studies

---

# Information Architecture

Financial services should be grouped logically.

Educational resources should support commercial services.

Navigation should reduce complexity.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Services
- Guides
- Calculators
- FAQs
- Contact

Educational resources should reinforce service pages.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization

- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- FAQPage
- Article

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Financial education
- Planning guides
- Tax resources
- Investment insights
- Regulatory updates

Accuracy should always outweigh publishing frequency.

---

# Local SEO

Important for advisory firms serving specific regions.

Include office locations and local contact information.

---

High Priority

- Licenses
- Professional memberships
- Case studies
- Testimonials (where permitted)

Medium Priority

- Publications
- Awards
- Speaking engagements
