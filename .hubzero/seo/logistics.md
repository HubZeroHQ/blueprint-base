# Logistics SEO

Logistics websites attract commercial customers by demonstrating operational capability, geographic coverage, and reliability.

SEO should focus on generating qualified business enquiries rather than maximizing general traffic.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find logistics providers
- Request quotations
- Track shipments
- Compare services
- Explore coverage

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Logistics
- Freight
- Shipping
- Warehousing
- Transportation
- Supply chain

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Services
- Industries
- Contact

Recommended

- Coverage
- Shipment Tracking
- Resources
- Facilities
- Careers

---

# Information Architecture

Services should be organized around industries and transportation methods.

Coverage information should remain easy to discover.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Services
- Industries
- Coverage
- Resources
- Contact

Educational content should reinforce service pages.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- LocalBusiness
- FAQPage
- Article

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Industry insights
- Supply chain resources
- Shipping guides
- Regulatory updates
- Case studies

Focus on expertise and operational capability.

---

# Local SEO

Important for regional logistics providers.

Highlight facilities, offices, and service areas.

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Certifications
- Coverage maps
- Fleet information
- Industry partnerships

Medium Priority

- Awards
- Operational statistics
- Testimonials

---

# Technical Considerations

- Fast loading
- Structured data
- Strong metadata
- Mobile-first
- Accessibility

---

# Common Mistakes

- Generic service descriptions
- Weak coverage information
- Hidden contact details
- Poor quotation flow
- Thin industry pages
