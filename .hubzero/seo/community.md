# Community SEO

Community websites grow through participation, valuable discussions, and long-term engagement.

SEO should attract people seeking knowledge, collaboration, or shared interests while encouraging them to become active members.

Community quality should always take precedence over rapid growth.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find communities
- Join discussions
- Solve problems
- Learn from others
- Attend events

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Communities
- Forums
- Discussions
- Topics
- Events
- Resources

---

Required

# Information Architecture

Content should be organized around meaningful topics and interests.

Navigation should encourage exploration while keeping discussions easy to discover.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Discussions
- Resources
- Events
- Member Profiles
- Categories

Popular discussions should naturally reinforce related resources.

Publish:

- Community highlights
