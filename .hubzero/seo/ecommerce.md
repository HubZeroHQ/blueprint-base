# Ecommerce SEO

Ecommerce websites grow through discoverability, product organization, and technical excellence.

SEO should improve how products are discovered, compared, and purchased.

Every product should be capable of generating organic traffic.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Find products
- Compare products
- Research purchases
- Check pricing
- Read reviews

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Product names
- Categories
- Brands
- Features
- Comparisons
- Buying intent

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Categories
- Product Pages
- Search
- About
- Contact

Recommended

- Buying Guides
- Brands
- FAQs
- Reviews
- Blog

---

# Information Architecture

Products should belong to logical categories.

Navigation should support exploration without overwhelming visitors.

Avoid unnecessary category depth.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Categories
- Products
- Related Products
- Buying Guides
- Brands

Cross-link naturally based on relevance.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Product
- Offer
- Organization
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Review
- AggregateRating
- FAQPage

---

# Content Strategy

Support products with educational content.

Examples:

- Buying guides
- Product comparisons
- Care guides
- Frequently asked questions
- Brand pages

---

# Local SEO

Relevant when physical stores exist.

Support:

- Store pages
- LocalBusiness schema
- Location information

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Reviews
- Ratings
- Product availability
- Shipping information
- Return policy

Medium Priority

- Certifications
- Guarantees
- Brand partnerships
