# Travel SEO

Travel websites attract visitors by helping them confidently plan destinations, experiences, and itineraries.

SEO should reduce uncertainty while inspiring exploration.

Content should support travelers throughout the planning process.

---

# Search Intent

Visitors typically search to:

- Explore destinations
- Compare travel options
- Plan itineraries
- Book experiences
- Learn travel requirements

---

# Primary Keyword Categories

- Destinations
- Attractions
- Tours
- Travel guides
- Hotels
- Activities

---

# Essential Landing Pages

Required

- Home
- Destinations
- Tours
- Contact

Recommended

- Itineraries
- Travel Guides
- Blog
- FAQs
- Booking

---

# Information Architecture

Destinations should organize the website.

Supporting content should naturally help visitors move toward booking.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Connect:

- Destinations
- Tours
- Guides
- Booking
- Related destinations

Encourage exploration across similar travel interests.

---

# Structured Data

Required

- Organization
- TouristDestination
- Website
- BreadcrumbList

Recommended

- Trip
- Event
- FAQPage
- Review

---

# Content Strategy

Publish:

- Destination guides
- Seasonal travel advice
- Local recommendations
- Packing guides
- Itineraries

Evergreen travel content should complement seasonal content.

---

# Local SEO

Important for regional operators.

Highlight offices, service areas, and departure locations.

---

# Trust Signals

High Priority

- Reviews
- Guides written by experts
- Professional photography
- Safety information

Medium Priority

- Partnerships
- Awards
- Travel certifications

---

# Technical Considerations

- Fast image delivery
- Mobile optimization
- Structured data
- Strong metadata

---

# Common Mistakes

- Outdated destination information
- Weak booking pages
- Duplicate travel guides
- Poor image optimization

---

# Success Metrics

- Booking enquiries
- Organic traffic
- Destination engagement
- Returning visitors
