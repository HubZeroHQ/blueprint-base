# Premium

Premium design communicates exceptional quality through restraint, craftsmanship, and attention to detail.

Unlike luxury, premium design is accessible rather than exclusive.

Quality should be evident without appearing extravagant.

---

# Core Principles

- Craftsmanship over extravagance
- Confidence over attention-seeking
- Detail over decoration
- Simplicity with refinement
- Quality through execution

---

# Typography

Typography should be refined and highly legible.

Hierarchy should feel balanced and deliberate.

---

# Layout

Layouts should feel spacious, calm, and carefully considered.

Every element should appear intentional.

---

# Color Philosophy

Color should reinforce sophistication through restraint.

Use accents sparingly to emphasize important actions.

---

# Shapes

Balanced proportions.

Moderate radius.

Soft but confident geometry.

---

# Spacing

Whitespace should create breathing room and emphasize quality.

Avoid visual clutter.

---

# Motion

Motion should feel smooth, subtle, and polished.

Avoid playful or exaggerated animations.

---

# Imagery

Professional photography, authentic products, and carefully composed visuals.

Avoid stock imagery whenever possible.

---

# Components

Components should prioritize usability while demonstrating attention to detail.

Small refinements matter more than visual complexity.

---

# Suitable Architectures

- Corporate
- Portfolio
- SaaS
- Healthcare
- Finance

---

# Avoid

Avoid visual excess, unnecessary effects, or design decisions that compete for attention.
