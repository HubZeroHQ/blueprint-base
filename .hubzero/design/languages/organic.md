# Organic

Organic design communicates warmth, humanity, and approachability.

Interfaces should feel natural, welcoming, and comfortable rather than mechanical.

Trust should emerge through empathy and simplicity.

---

# Core Principles

- Human-centered
- Calm
- Natural rhythm
- Gentle interactions
- Authentic communication

---

# Typography

Typography should feel approachable and easy to read.

Hierarchy should support relaxed reading experiences.

---

# Layout

Layouts should feel open and balanced.

Rigid symmetry is less important than natural flow.

---

# Color Philosophy

Colors should feel inspired by nature and real-world materials.

Avoid overly saturated or artificial palettes.

---

# Shapes

Soft geometry.

Rounded forms.

Comfortable proportions.

---

# Spacing

Generous spacing creates calm and improves readability.

---

# Motion

Motion should feel gentle and supportive.

Transitions should never distract.

---

# Imagery

Authentic people.

Natural environments.

Real experiences.

Avoid artificial stock photography.

---

# Components

Components should invite interaction without demanding attention.

Comfort should take precedence over visual novelty.

---

# Suitable Architectures

- Healthcare
- Education
- Nonprofit
- Hospitality
- Travel

---

# Avoid

Avoid harsh geometry, excessive contrast, aggressive motion, or highly technical aesthetics.
